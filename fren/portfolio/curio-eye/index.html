CURIO EYE for WeChat Official

Built from the ground up at CuriosityChina (A Farfetch Company), CURIO EYE is a companion app for WeChat Official Account managers.

Full Ownership

As the sole iOS developer on this project, I was responsible for:

  • Complete app development: Source code, resources, and architecture
  • App Store Optimization (ASO): Managed app store presence and keywords
  • Release Management: Bi-weekly release cycle planning and execution
  • Product Direction: Contributed to product decision-making
  • Bundle Optimization: Minimized app size and resource footprint

Technical Achievement

Wrote the entire application from scratch (v1.0 to v1.1.5), maintaining high code quality standards and delivering regular updates to meet business needs.

Technologies Used

Swift, UIKit, WeChat API, iOS SDK

Impact

Helped content creators and marketers manage their WeChat Official Accounts more effectively through native iOS features and intuitive interface design.
